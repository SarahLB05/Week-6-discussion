Easy Soft Jam Thumbprint Cookies

These are one of my favorite recipes to make when I want something easy and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 2/3 cup of sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of fruit jam

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg yolks and vanilla and mix well.
  4. Add the flour and salt and mix until dough forms.
  5. Roll the dough into small balls. Place dough balls on baking sheet with parchment paper
  6. Press a thumbprint or tablespoon into the center of each cookie.
  7. Fill the center with jam.
  8. Bake for about 10–12 minutes until lightly golden.
  9. Let cookies cool before serving.
